About RADIOGPT

We're pioneering the future of radio broadcasting through artificial intelligence, creating personalized audio experiences that adapt to every listener

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a team of AI researchers, music enthusiasts, and broadcast veterans, RADIOGPT emerged from a simple question: What if radio could truly understand and adapt to each listener?

Traditional radio has remained largely unchanged for decades, playing the same content to millions of listeners. We envisioned something different – a broadcasting platform powered by artificial intelligence that creates unique, personalized experiences for every individual.

Today, RADIOGPT serves over 1 million active listeners across 50+ AI-powered channels, using cutting-edge machine learning to curate perfect soundtracks for every moment of your day.
